chunk orginization:

we said we'd classify chunks into difficulties. instead of just easy, medium, and hard chunks, i added an extra one: starting chunks

these will appear at the beginning of the game and will be EXTREMELY easy, just to get new players used to the controls a bit.

as for how we will decided generation of difficulties, we should make it completely random. the player could get unlucky and get 3 hard chunks near the beginning. 

to fix this, i think we should introduce chunks in phases:
- 0 seconds: only starting chunks spawn
- 25 seconds: introduction of easy chunks: roughly 70% easy chunks and 30% starting chunks
- 60 seconds: introduction of medium chunks: perhaps 30% medium chunks and 70% easy chunks
- 120 seconds: introduction of hard chunks: perhaps 20% hard chunks, 50% medium chunks, and 30% easy chunks
- 160 seconds: 40% hard chunks, 40% medium chunks, and 20% easy chunks
- 210 seconds: 50% hard chunks, 40% medium chunks, and 10% easy chunks
